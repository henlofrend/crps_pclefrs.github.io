The Pain-related Cancer Late Effects Functional Rehabilitation Service (PCLEFRS): Referral & Pathway

PCLEFRS Referral Criteria

The referral criteria for PCLEFRS include:

  • Severe and persistent pain due to the consequences of cancer treatment (any tumour site)
  • Reduced physical function in a limb (including shoulder girdle)
  • Already seen by local pain, rehabilitation and/or late effects services without improvement in symptoms
  • Considered highly complex by local/regional services
  • Completed cancer treatment at least 12 months previously (excluding hormone therapy)

PCLEFRS is a tertiary service funded by NHS England. Any health professional can refer into the Service. Prior funding approval is not required for patients living in England; however patients living outside of England will need to obtain local area funding approval before a referral is submitted. Private referrals are also accepted. GPs should use the Electronic Referral System to make a referral. All other referral letters and enquiries should be sent to:
